Animation & Games.

I worked on my first animations at nine years old with my dad when he would get home from work. Back then I was working on 3dMax, the industry and tools have changed quite alot. I have done 3d, 2d, and Stop motion animation, they are all very fun mediums and something I see myself always dabbling with.

Photography.

The night is a beautiful place when captured with a long exposure... like 20 seconds or more. One of my all time favorite things is to pack a bag with props, a camera, flashlights and hit the road to some remote location. It's something I can do with friends or alone and have a great time. You get to see awesome locations, get insane pictures and almost always have a story to tell after.
